Free Art Friday Philadelphia

Free Art Friday PHL is a free art scavenger hunt in the city of Philadelphia. FAF PHL was formed to cultivate creativity, increase awareness in the art & design communities, and make works of art accessible to everyone.

Be sure to follow @fafphl and FAF PHL on Facebook for updates on Philly's first FAF.

How does it work?

Create

Artists, & Designers create custom pieces of art — anything from photos, to paitings, to prints, to sculptures.

Conceal

Artists hide their artwork around the city. After the artist hides their art, they post a photo clue to the FAF PHL Facebook page and tweet the same clue using #FAFPHL.

Consume

Anyone who finds a piece of art is free to take it! We only ask that you send a tweet or post to the FAF PHL Facebook page letting us know that the artwork has been found.
